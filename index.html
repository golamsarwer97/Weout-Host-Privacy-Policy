Privacy Policy for WeOut Host

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WeOut Host ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the WeOut Host mobile application (the "App").

This policy applies specifically to hosts, service providers, and business users utilizing the WeOut Host App to manage their experiences, bookings, and guests.

2. Information We Collect

In order to provide our services, we may collect the following types of information:

A. Personal and Business Information:

  • Account details: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Business information: Business name, registration details, address.
  • Verification documents: Government ID, business licenses required for platform security.

B. Device and App Usage Data:

  • Log data, diagnostic information, and generalized analytical data to help us improve app performance.
  • Push notification tokens to send you timely updates about bookings, payouts, and messages.

C. Permissions Requested via the App:

To function correctly, the App requires certain device permissions:

  • Camera & Photo Library (NSCameraUsageDescription / NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription): To allow you to take and upload profile pictures, business verification documents, and images for your experience listings.
  • Location Data (NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription): To accurately tag the locations of the experiences you host and provide localized mapping features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate your bookings, payout processing, and daily host operations.
  • To maintain account security, verify your identity, and prevent fraud.
  • To enable communication between you (the host) and your guests (e.g., via the in-app messaging system).
  • To send you important technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Companies that assist us with payment processing, cloud hosting (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud), and analytics.
  • With Guests: Only relevant public-facing profile and listing information (such as your name, profile photo, and business details) is shared with guests who book your experiences.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Account Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and personal data. You can initiate this request within the App settings or by contacting our support team directly. Upon request, your profile and associated documents will be securely deleted, except for data we are required to retain for legal/tax compliance.

7. Children's Privacy

The WeOut Host App is intended strictly for adults and business entities. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
